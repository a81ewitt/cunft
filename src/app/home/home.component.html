Cunft.app aims to give an easy way of creating NFTs. We hope to take NFTs beyond spiritless anthropoids and provide real world uses where unique, immutable records are required.
Cunt.app uses the Cardano blockchain and IPFS for its NFT creation and storage. Cardanos Proof Of Stake technology makes its more eco friendly than many of its competitors, meaning you can feel at ease not contributing to one of the worlds the largest emission sources.
